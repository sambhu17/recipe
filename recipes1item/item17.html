How to make Adai At Home

Ingredients

  • 1) Rice - ½ cup
  • 2) urad dhaal - ¼ cup
  • 3) chana dhaal - ¼ cup
  • 4) thoor dhaal - ¼ cup
  • 5) yellow moong dhaal - ¼ cup
  • 6) red chillis
  • 7) salt

    • Method

    Soak everything in water over night. Grind with little water.
