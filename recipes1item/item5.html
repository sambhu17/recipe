How to make Potato in Curd Gravy At Home

Ingredients

  • 1) 3 medium. potatoes boiled and peeled
  • 2) 1 cup curd or yogurt beaten
  • 3) 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
  • 4) 1 tablespoon salt
  • 5) 1/2 tablespoon dhania powder
  • 6) 1/4 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 7) 1/4 tablespoon garam masala
  • 8) 2 pinches asafoetida
  • 9) 1 stalk curry leaves
  • 10) 1 stalk curry leaves
  • 11) 1 1/4 cup water
  • 12) 1/2 tablespoon each ginger, garlic grated
  • 13) 2 green chillies slit
  • 14) 1 tablespoon each cumin, mustard seeds
  • 15) 1/4 tablespoon wheat flour
  • 16) 1 tablespoon oil

    • Method

    Cut potatoes into big pieces. Mash 3-4 pieces fine with hand. Keep both aside.

    Mix all the dry masala in 1/4 cup water.

    Heat oil. Add the seeds (cumin and mustard). When they splutter, add ginger-garlic, chilli and curry leaves.

    Add the masala mixture and fry for 2 minutes.

    Add beaten curd and fry for 5 minutes or till the curd loses its whiteness. Stir continuously after adding curd.


    Add the remaining water and all the potato and flour. Stir well.

    Boil and simmer for 10 minutes or till gravy thickens.

    Garnish with chopped coriander.

    Serve hot with thin wheat chappaties and rice.


    Making time: 30 minutes.

    Makes for: 5
