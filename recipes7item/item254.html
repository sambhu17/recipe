How to make Indian Cream of Tomato Soup At Home

Ingredients

  1. 1½ lb ripe tomatoes,chopped
  2. 1 tbsp chopped lemon grass
  3. 2 curry leaves
  4. 2" fresh ginger,peeled & chopped
  5. 1 tsp salt
  6. 4 fl oz water
  7. 4 fl oz single cream
  8. 1 pt milk
  9. 1 tsp ground roasted cumin seeds
  10. pinch ground black pepper
  11. pinch cayenne pepper
  12. 2 tsps lime or lemon juice
  13. 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

Method

Put the tomatoes,lemon grass, curry leaves,ginger, salt and water into a saucepan and bring to the boil. Lower the heat, cover and simmer gently for 15 minutes. Uncover, increase the heat and simmer more rapidly for another 15 minutes.Puree in a liquidiser then sieve. You should have about ¾ pt thick tomato juice.

Add the cream, milk, cumin seed, black and cayenne pepper and the lime or lemon juice. Stir together well and reheat gently without boiling. Serve garnished with freshly chopped coriander.
