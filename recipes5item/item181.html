How to make Ravva Dosa At Home

Required Ingredients:

  1. Yoghurt (preferably sour) -- 1 cup
  2. Rice flour -- 1/2 cup
  3. Moida -- 1/4 cup
  4. Semolina (Bombay Ravva) -- 1 cup
  5. Chopped Ginger -- 1 inch piece
  6. Chopped Coriander -- fistful
  7. Crushed Cashew nuts -- 15
  8. Finely Chopped Green Chilies -- 2 (about 1.5 tsp)
  9. Chopped Fresh Coconut -- 2 tbsp (very small pieces)
  10. Cumin Seeds -- 1 tsp
  11. Oil for cooking
  12. Salt to taste

Method

Mix everything except oil. Add enough amount of water and mix well such that when you pour batter on the frying pan, all you have to do is tilt the frying pan for the batter to spread out thinly and evenly. You shouldn't use an implement to spread the batter. It should spread on its own. It is a great snack. Allam Pacchadi (ginger pickle) goes well with this dish


Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Serves: 3-4
