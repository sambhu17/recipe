How to make Brown Gravy At Home

Ingredients

  1. 1 tomato grated or finely chopped
  2. 1 large onion cut in slivers
  3. 1 capsicum cut in thin lengths
  4. 1 tsp. garlic grated
  5. 1 tsp. ginger grated
  6. 1 tbsp. coriander leaves finely chopped
  7. 2 tbsp. tamarind water
  8. 1 tsp. wheat flour
  9. 1/2 tsp. red chilli powder
  10. 1/4 tsp. turmeric powder
  11. 1/2 tsp. dhania (coriander seed) powder
  12. 1/2 tsp. cumin seeds
  13. 3 pinches asafoetida
  14. salt to taste
  15. 3 tbsp. oil

Roast together and dry grind:

  1. 2 cardamoms
  2. 2 cloves
  3. 2 peppercorns
  4. 1/2 cinnamon stick broken
  5. 1 bayleaf
  6. 2 whole red chillies dry

Method

Dry roast wheat flour to a light pink, stirring continuously. Keep aside. Heat oil, add onions, fry till well browned. Drain well by pressing, putting back oil in pan. Cool onions a bit. Grind in mixie. Keep aside. In remaining hot oil, add cumin seeds allow to splutter. Add asafoetida, capsicum, ginger, garlic stir fry for a minute. Add all dry and ground masalas, flour, stir well.

Add tomatoes, tamarind water, salt, stir. Simmer till gravy is thick and oil starts to separate. Add prepared veggies and stir, keep cooking for 2 minutes. Garnish with chopped coriander, serve with parathas, rotis, etc.

Goes well with: Chunky veggies like boiled baby potatoes with skin, boiled peas, chopped stirfried brinjal pieces. (For this, fry brinjals pieces in oil, drain, keep aside, adjust quantity of oil required, and continue by frying the onions.)
