How to make Lentil and Vegetable Broth At Home

Ingredients

For the stock

  1. 2 tablespoones moong dal
  2. 2 onions
  3. 2 large tomatoes

For the topping

  1. 1 chopped onion
  2. ½ teacup shredded cabbage
  3. ½ teacup chopped spinach
  4. 2 tablespoons baked beans or tomato ketchup
  5. 1 chopped tomato
  6. 1 tablespoon oil salt and pepper to taste grated cheese to serve

Method

For the stock

1. Cut the onions and tomatoes into big pieces.

2. Add the moong dal and 6 teacups of water and cook in a pressure cooker.

3. When cooked, blend in a liquidiser and strain.

How to proceed

1. Heat the oil and fry the onion for 1 minute.

2. Add the vegetables and fry again for 1 minute.

3. Add the stock and boil for 10 minutes.

4. Add the beans, tomato, salt and pepper and give one boil.


Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Serves 6to 8.
