How to make Butter chicken At Home

Cooking alone your favourite butter chicken is an emotion! whereas butter chicken is for soul's taste buds. Dost: Aunty your butter chicken recipe is out of this world.. I've never eaten something like this ever in my life.. would love to come over again sometime for a new dish!!

Indgredients: (serves 6)

  • 1) 3 lbs. chicken drumsticks/ thighs/ sliced breast pieces
  • 2) 1 tbsp. oil
  • 3) 1 tsp. ginger p1 tsp. garlic pasteaste
  • 4) 3 tsp. chilli powder (optional)
  • 5) 1 cup yogurt or buttermilk
  • 6) 1 cup sour cream
  • 7) 1/2 tomato puree
  • 8) 4 oz. butter
  • 9) 6 cardamoms
  • 10) 6 cloves
  • 11) 2 sticks cinnamon
  • 13) 3 tsp. salt or to taste

    • Method

    Heat the oil in a large saucepan. Fry the ginger, garlic, cardamoms, cinnamon and cloves on medium low heat for a minute, and add the chicken with the yogurt or buttermilk, tomato puree, sour cream, chilli powder and salt. Cook on medium low heat, stirring occasionally, for half an hour, keeping the saucepan covered with a lid. Add butter before serving.
