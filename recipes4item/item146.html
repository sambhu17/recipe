How to make Pepper Rasam At Home

Ingredients

  1. 1 small tomato, chopped into small cubes
  2. 1 1/2 tsp peppercorns
  3. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  4. 1 tsp mustard seeds
  5. 1 sprig curry leaves
  6. 2 tsp thick tamarind juice
  7. 1/2 tsp sugar or jaggery water
  8. 1 tsp chopped coriander leaves
  9. A pinch of hing(asoefetida)
  10. Salt to taste
  11. 2 tsp ghee(clarified butter)
  12. 6 cups of water

Method

Powder the peppercorns in a peppermill or coffee grinder to get a very coarse powder. Mix together the water, tomato, pepper,salt sugar(jaggery) and tamarind juice. Bring it to a boil on a low flame.

Season with mustard, hing, curry leaves and cumin in ghee. Garnish with chopped coriander. Eat when hot with plain rice.
